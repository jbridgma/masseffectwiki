Cadmium (Cd) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A metal often used in constructing electronic components and in complex manufacturing.

Acquisition[]
Cadmium can be detected via orbital scan and may be discovered in small amounts on foot. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where cadmium can be found on foot: 

Eos
Kadara
Voeld
Specific Uses[]
Cadmium is used for a large number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Cadmium listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
