Acquisition[]
Mods are acquired and upgraded through purchases of packs and items in the multiplayer Store.

Basic - Shotgun Receiver: Capacitor boosts kinetic coil generators, increasing shot penetration through solid objects.
Note:  The Venom shotgun is unable to equip a Receiver Mod.
Note:  Shotgun penetration damage is not reflected in the displayed weapon stats.
