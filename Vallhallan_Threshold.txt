The Vallhallan Threshold is a Terminus Systems cluster at the outer rim of the galaxy. Sparse in terms of population, the only known activity in the cluster comes from pirates, terrorists, and concentrated corporate interests on Garvug. The quarian Migrant Fleet is in the general area in 2185, mining nearby worlds for resources.

Trivia[]
The Vallhallan Threshold is most likely named after Valhalla, the afterlife of Norse mythology.
The name of the cluster was adjusted in-game in accordance to the more common form "Valhalla" in Mass Effect 3.
