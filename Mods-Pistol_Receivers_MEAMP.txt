Acquisition[]
Mods are acquired and upgraded through purchases of packs and items in the multiplayer Store.

Basic - Pistol Receiver: Capacitor boosts kinetic coil generators, increasing shot penetration through solid objects.
Note:  The Scorpion pistol is unable to equip a Receiver Mod.
Note:  Pistol penetration damage is not reflected in the displayed weapon stats.
