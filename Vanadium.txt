Vanadium (V) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
This rare metal can be used to produce various construction alloys.

Acquisition[]
Vanadium can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants.  
Planets where vanadium can be mined: 

Eos
Planets where vanadium can be found on foot: 

Elaaden
Specific Uses[]
Vanadium is used for a huge number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Vanadium listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
