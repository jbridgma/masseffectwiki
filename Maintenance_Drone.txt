Maintenance Drones are used by the Shadow Broker to maintain his ship in the violent atmosphere of Hagalaz. They are employed to maintain the hull and remove debris from the storm.

Capabilities[]
Offensive:
Maintenance Drones only have the shock attack similar to Combat Drones.
Defensive:
Maintenance Drones only have minimal health.

Tactics[]
These drones are very easy to destroy at any range and their attacks are weak, so they aren't that big of a threat.
