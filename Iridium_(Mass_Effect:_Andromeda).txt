Iridium (Ir) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A hard, ultra-dense metallic element that is highly resistant to heat and corrosion.

Acquisition[]
Iridium can be detected via orbital scan and may be discovered in small amounts on foot. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where iridium can be found on foot: 

Kadara
Specific Uses[]
Iridium is used for a small number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Iridium listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
