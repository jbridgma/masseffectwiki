Silicon (Si) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A metalloid element useful in electronics manufacturing and the fabrication of ceramic compounds.

Acquisition[]
Silicon can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where silicon can be mined: 

Elaaden
H-047c
Specific Uses[]
Silicon is used for a large number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Silicon listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
