



Mercenary Armor is produced by Ariake Technologies for humans, turians and krogan in light, medium and heavy versions. It is also Urdnot Wrex's default armor. It uses ablative weave technology, which increases shield capacity but also lowers protection against biotic and tech damage in comparison to other armors.[1]
