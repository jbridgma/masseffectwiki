Armor Merchant

Race
Angara


Gender
Male


Appearances
Mass Effect: Andromeda


Oniijev is a merchant on Aya. He typically sells angaran armors on the Marketplace, and can often be overheard encouraging people to "invest in personal protection". 
Oniijev is welcoming of new clientele and is confident on the quality of his wares. If he doesn't have something, he claims to be able to procure it by the next day.
