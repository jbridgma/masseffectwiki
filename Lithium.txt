Lithium (Li) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A useful light metal used in multiple engineering applications, including vehicle batteries, radiation shields, and high-quality steel production. 

Acquisition[]
Lithium can be detected via orbital scan and may be discovered in small amounts on foot. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where lithium can be found on foot: 

Kadara
Voeld
Specific Uses[]
Lithium is used for a medium number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Lithium listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
