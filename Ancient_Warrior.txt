


Ancient Warrior is a passive class power in Mass Effect 3's multiplayer mode. 

Power Ranks[]
Rank 1: Ancient Warrior[]
Boost health, shields/barriers, melee damage, and durability.
Utilize heavy melee to switch to the powerful Ascension Stance to increase damage and power recharge speed at the expense of increasing the damage taken. This stance lasts 45 seconds.

Health & Shield Bonus: 15%
Melee Damage Bonus: 15%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 10%
Ascension Recharge Speed: 5%
Ascension Damage Penalty: 15%


Rank 2: Durability[]
Increase health and shield bonuses by 10%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25%
Melee Damage Bonus: 15%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 10%
Ascension Recharge Speed: 5%
Ascension Damage Penalty: 15%


Rank 3: Melee Damage[]
Increase melee damage by 20%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25%
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 10%
Ascension Recharge Speed: 5%
Ascension Damage Penalty: 15%


Rank 4: Ascension Damage Bonus/Durability[]



Ascension Damage Bonus
Increase damage bonus by 5% while Ascension Stance is active.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25%
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 15%
Ascension Recharge Speed: 5%
Ascension Damage Penalty: 15%


Durability
Increase health and shield bonuses by 15%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 40%
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 10%
Ascension Recharge Speed: 5%
Ascension Damage Penalty: 15%



Rank 5: Ascension Recharge Speed/Shield Recharge[]



Ascension Recharge Speed
Increase recharge speed by 10% while Ascension Stance is active.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25% (Ascension Damage Bonus), 40% (Durability)
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 15% (Ascension Damage Bonus), 10% (Durability)
Ascension Recharge Speed: 15%
Ascension Damage Penalty: 15%


Shield Recharge
Decrease shield recharge delay by 15%




Rank 6: Ascension Mastery/Fitness Expert[]



Ascension Mastery
Increase damage by 10% and recharge speed by 10% while Ascension Stance is active at the expense of damage taken increasing by 10%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25% (Ascension Damage Bonus), 40% (Durability)
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 25% (Ascension Damage Bonus), 20% (Durability)
Ascension Recharge Speed: 25% (Ascension Recharge Speed), 15% (Shield Recharge)
Ascension Damage Penalty: 25%


Fitness Expert
Increase health and shield bonuses by 25%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 50% (Ascension Damage Bonus), 65% (Durability)
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Ascension Damage Bonus: 15% (Ascension Damage Bonus), 10% (Durability)
Ascension Recharge Speed: 25% (Ascension Recharge Speed), 15% (Shield Recharge)
Ascension Damage Penalty: 15%



Player Notes[]
The damage penalties from Ascension mode can be negated if Seeker Swarm is evolved for Damage Reduction.
