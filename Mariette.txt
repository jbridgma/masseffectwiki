Mass Effect: Andromeda


Mariette is a colonist that has traveled to Andromeda on the Nexus.
When the Pathfinder first visits the Nexus, Mariette can be found in the Militia Office in Nexus Operations. She is married to Nilken Rensus who is beeing held by Nexus security on murder charges. She believes her husband innocent and does not trust the Nexus offcials to judge the case impartial. She therefore asks the Pathfinder to speak with her husband.
