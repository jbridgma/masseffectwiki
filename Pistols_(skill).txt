This article is about the skill in Mass Effect: Andromeda. For pistol weapons in Mass Effect, see Pistols.

Pistols is a passive Combat skill in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Skill Ranks[]
Improves sidearm training for pistols (including submachine guns). Pistols are designed largely for short to mid-range combat and shooting on the move.

Player Notes[]
Mechanics
Pistols is a passive skill that is always active.
Strategies
The Rank 5 Reload upgrade does not affect the cooldown period of a weapon that uses the Vintage Heat Sink.
