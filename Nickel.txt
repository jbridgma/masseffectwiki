Nickel (Ni) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A corrosion-resistant transition metal useful for manufacturing purposes.

Acquisition[]
Nickel can be detected via orbital scan and may be discovered in small amounts on foot. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where Nickel can be mined: 

Havarl
Planets where Nickel can be found on foot: 

Elaaden
H-047c
Havarl
Specific Uses[]
Nickel is used for a large number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Nickel listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
