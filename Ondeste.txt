Prerequisite: Horizon (mission) (Mass Effect 2)


Ondeste is a dim red dwarf with three terrestrial planets, rating good to rich in resources required by the Normandy SR-2, particularly platinum and palladium. Only Zesmeni has commercial exploitation from asari mining concerns while the others barely warrant further scientific or material interest.
