Analysis[]
The chitin of certain Heleus creatures is constantly strengthened and repaired by colonies of microorganisms. Study could lead to breakthroughs in materials technology. 

Acquisition[]
Spitbugs, shemrys, and pallads can be hunted in the wild for their chitinous exoskeletons. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 

Specific Uses[]
Soft Chitin is used for a small number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Soft Chitin listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
