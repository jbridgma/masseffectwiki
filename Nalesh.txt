Nalesh is a system northeast from the Heleus Cluster's Black Hole. It is one FTL jump away from Nol and Hefena.
According to Jaal, Mineral wealth from Nalesh once fueled angaran industries on Voeld and other territories. Suvi is hopeful that it will be the case once again.
