Renderable Plates is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
The tissues of these animal plates can be rendered into a variety of adhesives and lubricants at a fraction of the cost of producing synthetic equivalents. 

Acquisition[]
Adhis, eirochs, taurgs, and galorns can be hunted for their plates. Plates can also be purchased from certain merchants.

Specific Uses[]
Renderable Plates is used for a small number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Renderable Plates listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
