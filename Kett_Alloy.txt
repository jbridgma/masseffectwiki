Kett Alloy is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
The kett utilize a number of unfamiliar alloys in their technology. At this time, the Initiative is unable to replicate most of them. 

Acquisition[]
These alloys can be acquired by defeating kett enemies, or by dismantling their weapons and armor. They can also be purchased from certain merchants. 

Specific Uses[]
Kett Alloy is used for a medium number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Kett Alloy listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
