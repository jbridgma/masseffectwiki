


The Mass Effect Library Edition is currently composed of two anthology volumes in hardcover editions. Published by Dark Horse Comics, each volume collects all of the Mass Effect series comics released up to a certain point.
Both volumes have also been republished in trade paperback as the Mass Effect Omnibus. 

Volume 1[]
The first volume contains all issues released up to and including August 2012 in a single collection. It was published on May 15, 2013.[1]
The collection contains the following comics:

Mass Effect: Redemption
Mass Effect: Evolution
Mass Effect: Invasion
Mass Effect: Homeworlds
Mass Effect: Incursion
Mass Effect: Inquisition
Mass Effect: Conviction
The Omnibus edition uses the cover image for Mass Effect: Redemption 1. It was published on November 16, 2016. [2]


Volume 2[]
Volume 2 contains the other remaining issues connected to the original trilogy prior to the publication of Mass Effect: Andromeda-related media. It was released on May 19, 2015.[3]
The collection contains the following comics:

Mass Effect: Foundation
Mass Effect: He Who Laughs Best
Blasto: Eternity is Forever
The Omnibus edition uses the cover image for Mass Effect: Foundation 3. It was published on February 8, 2017. [4]
