Description[]
Outlaws cobbled together the compact Shorty because they wanted a close-range weapon with extra ammo. The shogun fires three-round bursts, but is heavy to carry because of a large ammunition clip; the recoil kicks like an angry mule.

Features[]
The displayed damage is per round, meaning the actual damage dealt is thrice the stated number if it all hits.

Strategies[]
Even unupgraded, the Shorty is a good fit for krogan classes due to their easy access to Rage Rank 2: +25% weapon weight capacity. Most krogan classes rely on Rage to rack up kills and shine in the battlefield, though other characters (and alternative skill builds) can also take the weapon provided the proper points in weight capacity have been invested.
