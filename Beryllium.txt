Beryllium (Be) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
An ideal metal for lightweight construction in a variety of applications. Useful as an alloying element. 

Acquisition[]
Beryllium can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where beryllium can be mined: 

Kadara
Voeld
Specific Uses[]
Beryllium is used for a medium number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Beryllium listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
