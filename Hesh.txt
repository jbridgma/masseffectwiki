Description[]
The semi-automatic Hesh, translated to "order," is a kett weapon that fires clusters of plasma-surrounded projectiles. Fired rounds are powerful enough to stagger most foes and demand submission from the rest.

Features[]
Singleplayer Hesh Ranks I - IX uses Vanadium for development while Rank X uses Element Zero.
