







The following information is drawn from cut content and is not to be considered canon in any way.

The music in the first Mass Effect game, in addition to having gone through multiple iterations, has also be noted for the many unused tracks present in the game files that cannot be heard in the game. Many of them are variations of themes that are heard in the game, or on the Mass Effect Original Soundtrack, and a few have been reused for Mass Effect 3.
