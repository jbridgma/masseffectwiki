Description[]
The Silhesh, translated to "disorder," is a smaller version of the Thokin assault rifle used by kett fighters in the field. Like the Thokin, the Silhesh SMG allows the kett to shoot tracking-enabled, plasma-surrounded rounds with a lightning-quick rate of fire.

Features[]
The pistol gained improvements to damage in its sole documented multiplayer update.
