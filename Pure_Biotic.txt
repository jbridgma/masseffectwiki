






This article is about Liara T'Soni's class power in Mass Effect 3. For her class and class talent in Mass Effect and her class power in Mass Effect 2, see Asari Scientist.


Pure Biotic is a passive class power in Mass Effect 3's single-player mode.

See also: Squad Members Guide (Mass Effect 3)#Liara T'Soni

Boost biotic and defensive abilities.

Weapon Damage

Squad Bonus

Singularity Recharge
Increase recharge speed of Liara's Singularity by 100%.




Player Notes[]
The Rank 6 Squad Bonus evolution applies to all members of the party with biotic powers including Liara. There is possibility for confusion, as similarly named "Squad Bonus" evolutions on Shepard's various class powers do not apply to Shepard, only to NPC squadmates.
