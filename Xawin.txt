Description[]
WARNING: Level 1 Cold Hazard
Xawin has a thin atmosphere of carbon dioxide and ethane. The surface is frozen, and mainly composed of cobalt with deposits of copper. Planetside conditions are hazardous, with constant ice storms wracking the surface.
