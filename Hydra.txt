Prerequisite: Priority: Geth Dreadnought (Mass Effect 3)

Hydra has two gas giants, three rocky planets, and no hospitable worlds, though that hasn't stopped primitive life from evolving there. The terrestrial worlds are either scorching hot or freezing cold, with no in-betweens.

The system may be named after the Lernaean Hydra, a monster from Greek mythology with nine heads that was killed by Hercules during his Twelve Labors.
