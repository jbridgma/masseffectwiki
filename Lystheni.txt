


The Lystheni are an "offshoot" of the salarians, unwelcome in Council space. They may be found living among batarians, exiled quarians, and other galactic refuse at the Omega station. Nothing more is known about them.[1]






The following information is drawn from cut content and is not to be considered canon in any way.

Cut Content[]
Main article: Lystheni (Cut)
The lystheni were intended to have a major role during the Mass Effect cut assignment Global Plot 3, where they would have been waging a war against the hanar.
