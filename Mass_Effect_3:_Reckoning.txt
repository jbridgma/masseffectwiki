



Mass Effect 3: Reckoning is a DLC pack for Mass Effect 3's multiplayer mode. It adds new characters, weapons, and equipment. The pack was announced on February 21, 2013 and was released on February 26, 2013 worldwide for Xbox 360 and PC; for PlayStation 3, it was released February 26, 2013 worldwide except in Europe, where it was released on February 27, 2013. The pack is available at no additional cost. Mass Effect 3: Reckoning is the last multiplayer DLC for Mass Effect 3.[1][2]

Description[]
Prepare for the Reckoning! Newfound allies join the multiplayer war for survival in Mass Effect 3: Reckoning. The Geth Juggernaut, Cabal Vanguard, Talon Mercenary, Alliance Infiltration Unit, and more take up arms to stop the Reaper threat. Smash your enemies with the Biotic Hammer as you lay waste to the battlefield as the Krogan Warlord.
Wield 7 new weapons for multiplayer, including the Geth Spitfire Assault Rifle, Venom Shotgun, Lancer Assault Rifle, and amplify your arsenal with new equipment and weapon mods featuring the Geth Scanner and Assault Rifle Ultralight materials.
