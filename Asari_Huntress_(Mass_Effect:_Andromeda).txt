Mobile fighter with biotic powers who can cloak allies.
In the Milky Way, the Asari Huntress was a well-regarded bounty hunter until she took the wrong job and found herself on the most-wanted list. She joined the Andromeda Initiative to escape her enemies and begin the hunt anew.
