




Cybernetic Device






The Cybernetic Device is a  special item resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Description[]
This device, and its accompanying software, will compensate for the damage to Alain Nigh's spinal implants, enabling them to function correctly.

Specific Uses[]
The cybernetic device is used during The Firefighters.
