2.1 Deliver The Flower To Kesh


3 Rewards


Acquisition[]
Speak to Vorn in the garden area of the lower section of the New Tuchanka colony on Elaaden. This mission unlocks after completing Nakmor Drack's loyalty mission.

Walkthrough[]
Deliver The Flower To Kesh[]
Deliver the flower to Nakmor Kesh in her office in the Operations area of the Nexus. She is not interested and Ryder can offer to take the flower back or scold her. This decision has no impact and concludes the mission.

Rewards[]
+270 XP (might not show on screen)
+29 AVP
