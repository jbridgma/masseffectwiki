Player Notes[]
EDI's Rank 6 Squad Bonus evolution applies to all members of the party with tech powers including EDI herself. There is possibility for confusion, as similarly named "Squad Bonus" evolutions on Shepard's various class powers do not apply to Shepard, only to NPC squadmates.
