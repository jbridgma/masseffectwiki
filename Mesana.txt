Prerequisite: Priority: The Citadel II (Mass Effect 3)

Mesana is a remote system with two asari colonies. One is being mined for resources despite being inhospitable, and the other has a breathable environment but used as a place of exile for asari with Ardat-Yakshi predispositions.
