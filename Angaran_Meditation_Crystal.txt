Angaran Meditation Crystal is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
An apparently delicate crystal incorporating angaran technology to produce an electromagnetic field. The field reacts soothingly with angaran biology but has other applications as well. 

Acquisition[]
Angaran meditation crystals can be found in areas frequented by the angara. These crystals can also be purchased from certain merchants. 

Specific Uses[]
Angaran Meditation Crystal is used for a small number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Angaran Meditation Crystal listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
