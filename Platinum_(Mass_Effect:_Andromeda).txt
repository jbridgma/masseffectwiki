Platinum (Pt) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
Platinum is a malleable noble metal with multiple catalytic applications. Also possesses a degree of cultural cachet among humans. 

Acquisition[]
Platinum can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where platinum can be mined: 

H-047c
Voeld
Planets where platinum can be found on foot:

H-047c
Havarl
Comet Dawnstar rewards a small amout of platinum when scanned.

Specific Uses[]
Platinum is used for a large number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Platinum listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
