


Merixus needs an angaran ionic filter to improve production of purified water on Elaaden. He still hasn't been allowed into the city, and has asked you to track down the filter for him.

Acquisition[]
Merixus will be available on a pad on the left side of the Docks in Aya once Pathfinder Ryder has secured an Initiative presence in the city.
Note: This is the third outpost supplies mission given by Merixus. The first outpost supplies mission given is Task: Outpost Supplies on Eos, the second is Task: Outpost Supplies on Kadara, and the third is Task: Outpost Supplies on Elaaden. When returning to Merixus after completing any (or all) of the outpost supplies missions, the last supplies mission Task: Outpost Supplies on Voeld will be given.

Walkthrough[]
Acquire A Ionic Filter On Aya[]
Purchase an ionic water filter for 350  on Aya from the Aqueduct Engineer at the navpoint east of the Marketplace (down the staircase near the Holographic Message Terminal).

Deliver The Ionic Filter To Merixus[]
Deliver the filter to Merixus on the Docks.

Rewards[]
+270 XP
+29 AVP 
+2% Elaaden viability
