Description[]
Xathorron is a small rock planet, with a thin atmosphere of carbon dioxide and hydrogen sulfide. The surface is scorching hot, and mainly composed of sulfur with deposits of boron.

Additional Information[]
The planet's moons are an uncommon source of rhodamine. The dye can be found in mineral deposits.
