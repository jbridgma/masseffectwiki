


Liberator Armor is an armor produced by Devlon Industries. It provides light to moderate protection.
It is environmentally sealed: if any member of the squad is present and wearing Explorer Armor, it will negate the timer on environments with Level 1 environmental hazards and slow down the timer on Level 2. However, in consequence, all of the armor's stats are lower than most armors produced by other manufacturers.[1]
