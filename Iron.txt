Iron (Fe) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A common metallic element found in many planetary cores. Broadly useful for multiple engineering purposes. 

Acquisition[]
Iron can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where iron can be mined: 

Elaaden
Eos
H-047c
Specific Uses[]
Iron is used for a large number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Iron listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
