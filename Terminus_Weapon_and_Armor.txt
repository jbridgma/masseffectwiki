


The Terminus Weapon and Armor is a DLC pack for Mass Effect 2. This pack was available as a retail bonus item to players who pre-ordered the game from certain retailers.

Content[]
One armor set:
Terminus Assault Armor
One weapon:
M-490 Blackstorm
Pricing[]
The Terminus Weapon and Armor pack was available for free to Xbox 360 and PC owners who pre-ordered Mass Effect 2 from Gamestop or Play.com. It is now available on Origin free of charge for PC owners. On PlayStation 3, it is available for $1.99 USD or in the N7 Complete Arsenal Bundle.
