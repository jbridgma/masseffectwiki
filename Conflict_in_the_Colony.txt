Acquisition[]
Prerequisite: Elaaden: A New World

This mission starts automatically on the first visit to Elaaden groundside. 

Walkthrough[]
The goal is simple: follow the navpoints and talk to the person at the end.

Visit the Paradise[]
The generated navpoint is in The Paradise. A new navpoint will be added on approach.

Locate a krogan named Jorgal Strux[]
Head to the navpoint to see the krogan. On approach to the shuttle pad right outside Paradise his Jorgal clansmen declare their wish to have Strux run things.

Speak to Strux[]
Strux reveals that a krogan named Nakmor Morda has become a tyrant and intends to destroy the Nexus - among other things. Nakmor Drack trusts Morda and doesn't believe this. Strux advises Pathfinder Ryder to visit the krogan colony New Tuchanka and talk to their inside guy Ravanor Brenk for proof.
The mission completes after the conversation and the follow-up mission Parlay with the Krogan starts automatically.

Rewards[]
+270 XP
+29 AVP 
+2% Elaaden viability
