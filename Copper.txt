Copper (Cu) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
Copper is a noble metal with high conductivity that makes it useful for a variety of purposes. 

Acquisition[]
Copper can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where copper can be mined: 

Kadara
Voeld
Specific Uses[]
Copper is used for a medium number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Copper listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
