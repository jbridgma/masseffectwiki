


The Recon Operations Pack is a DLC pack for Mass Effect 2 released on January 18, 2011, and is only available on the PlayStation Network.

Content[]
Collector Armor
Collector Assault Rifle
Recon Hood
Sentry Interface
Umbra Visor
Pricing[]
The pack is available on the PlayStation Network for $1.99 USD. It is also included in the N7 Complete Arsenal Bundle.
