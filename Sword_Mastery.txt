


Sword Mastery is a passive class power in Mass Effect 3's multiplayer mode.

Power Ranks[]
Rank 1: Sword Mastery[]
Boost health, shields, melee damage, durability, and movement speed.

Health & Shield Bonus: 20%
Melee Damage Bonus: 15%
Movement Speed Bonus: 10%


Rank 2: Durability[]
Increase health and shield bonuses by 15%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 35%
Melee Damage Bonus: 15%
Movement Speed Bonus: 10%


Rank 3: Sword Damage[]
Increase sword damage by 20%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 35%
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Movement Speed Bonus: 10%


Rank 4: Sword Damage/Durability[]



Sword Damage
Increase sword damage by 30%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 35%
Melee Damage Bonus: 65%
Movement Speed Bonus: 10%


Durability
Increase health and shield bonuses by 20%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 55%
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%
Movement Speed Bonus: 10%



Rank 5: Martial Artist/Shield Recharge & Speed[]



Martial Artist
Increase sword damage by 50% for 20 seconds after an enemy is killed by a sword attack.



Shield Recharge & Speed
Decrease shield-recharge delay by 10%.Increase movement speed by 10%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 35% (Sword Damage), 55% (Durability)
Melee Damage Bonus: 65% (Sword Damage), 35% (Durability)
Movement Speed Bonus: 20%



Rank 6: Shield Damage/Armor Damage[]



Shield Damage
Increase sword damage to shields/barrier by 50%.



Armor Damage
Increase sword damage to armor by 50%.




Player Notes[]
"Sword Damage" and "Martial Artist" apply not only to the Shadow's melee, but her Electric Slash and Shadow Strike powers as well.
