


The Mass Effect 2 Limited Collectors' Edition is offered through participating retailers.
It includes the following bonus materials:

A behind-the-scenes and making of DVD
A 48-page hardcover "Art of Mass Effect 2" art book
Limited edition issue #1 of Mass Effect: Redemption
Unique items, including special Collectors' Edition armor and weapons, and content
Limited Edition Cerberus Network card granting access to bonus content (this also comes with the standard edition)
All housed in a premium tin package with exclusive artwork
