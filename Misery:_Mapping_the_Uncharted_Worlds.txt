Misery: Mapping the Uncharted Worlds was an assignment in Mass Effect which was cut during development. It would involve mapping different planets.

Plotline[]
The assignment would start with Commander Shepard offering to help a certain character. Misery itself would also need to be mapped, and in addition, Shepard could help map three other worlds. A character named "Gem" could also be asked about something at some point.[1]
