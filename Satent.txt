Prerequisite: Star Chart from Baria Frontiers store (Mass Effect 2)

Development in Satent is spurred by Boro being bootstrapped as a volus colony world. Terminus Systems pirates attempt to take advantage, though the volus counter this with turian military response.
The terrestrial worlds are particularly rich in resources, and two out of the three even have element zero deposits.
