Description[]
One of Arcturus' hydrogen-helium gas giants, Eirene had infrastructure in place for fueling civilian and military starships with helium-3 and antiprotons. None of this infrastructure has survived the Reapers.

Trivia[]
Eirene is named after the Greek name for "Peace" (Greek: Ειρήνη).
