The Sweeper is an assault rifle in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Description[]
The Sweeper—a particle weapon that fires three-shot bursts—is largely a mystery to its own developers. The weapon is derived from Remnant technology, then repurposed for Andromeda Initiative needs. It does not require reloading, but is prone to overheating. Sweepers should be turned over for study between missions.

Features[]
The displayed damage is per shot, and the total damage noted to be thrice the stated value in the Mass Effect: Andromeda APEX HQ app.
The Sweeper received multiple changes to damage during multiplayer updates. Its recoil has also been reduced, and the hit reaction to the weapon also changed to a short interrupt.
