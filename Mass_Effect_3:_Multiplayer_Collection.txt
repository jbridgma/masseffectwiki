


Mass Effect 3: Multiplayer Collection is a DLC bundle pack for Mass Effect 3 released on March 19, 2013. It contains all five downloadable Mass Effect 3 Multiplayer content packs and is only available on PlayStation Network.

Content[]
Mass Effect 3: Resurgence Pack
Mass Effect 3: Rebellion Pack
Mass Effect 3: Earth
Mass Effect 3: Retaliation
Mass Effect 3: Reckoning
Pricing[]
The Multiplayer Collection is available for free on PlayStation Network.
