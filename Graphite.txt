Graphite (C) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
This crystalline carbon mineral is an electrical conductor and a useful part of many manufacturing processes.

Acquisition[]
Graphite can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where graphite can be mined: 

Elaaden
Eos
Specific Uses[]
Graphite is used for a small number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Graphite listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
