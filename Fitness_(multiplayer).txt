






This article is about the Fitness power for multiplayer classes in Mass Effect 3. For an overview of the base power in Mass Effect 3, see Fitness (power). For the Talent in Mass Effect, see Fitness.

Fitness is a passive class power in Mass Effect 3's multiplayer mode. 
Certain races and classes have specialized versions of Fitness detailed here.
