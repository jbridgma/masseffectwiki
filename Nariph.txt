Nariph is a gateway system. Its giant planets are believed to be extrasolar captures. They have moderate resources, although poorly developed. The only infrastructures are on Jonus, serving as water and fuel depots for the entire Pylos Nebula.

Trivia[]
One of the captain's logs on the MSV Broken Arrow refers to a "Nhiaryph" system as a shore leave destination. It is not clear if this is an alternate name for Nariph, a spelling error, or an entirely different but similarly-named system.
