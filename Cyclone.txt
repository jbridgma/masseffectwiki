The Cyclone is an assault rifle in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Description[]
The Cyclone's rapid rate of fire requires a short warm up before use. The rifle's mediocre accuracy is balanced by a massive capacity. As its manufacturer states, "With so many rounds to spare, what does it matter if a few don't land?" The Cyclone was removed from production years prior to the Andromeda Initiative's departure due to intense maintenance requirements, but some soldiers still swear by it. 

Features[]
The Cyclone has strong recoil and may need stability mods for optimal use.
Vetra Nyx wields a Cyclone as her weapon of choice.

Strategies[]
Combined with a Beam Emitter augmentation, the Cyclone turns into an armor destroyer. This combination is an excellent option for taking down Destroyers, Fiends, and Hydras.
The Turian Havoc Trooper has a particular affinity for weapons with spin-up times like the Cyclone, turning it into a hovering infantry shredder given the right skillset.
