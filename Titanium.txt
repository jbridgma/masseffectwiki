Titanium (Ti) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
This extremely strong transition metal vital for manufacturing starship components and other equipment. 

Acquisition[]
Titanium can be detected via orbital scan and mined with the Nomad. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where titanium can be mined: 

H-047c
Kadara
Planets where titanium can be found on foot: 

H-047c
Specific Uses[]
Titanium is used for a medium number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Titanium listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
