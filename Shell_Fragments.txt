Shell Filaments is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A keratin-like structure forms the basis of shell plating on certain creatures. If replicated, this biopolymer could prove useful in kinetic padding. 

Acquisition[]
Kaerkyns, dralls, taurgs, and echidnas can be hunted in the wild for their shell filaments. Additionally, shell plating can be purchased from certain merchants. 

Specific Uses[]
Shell Filaments is used for a small number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Shell Filaments listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
