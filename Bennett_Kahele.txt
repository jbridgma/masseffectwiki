Bennett Kahele is a human member of the Andromeda Initiative. 







“Make no mistake — this is a one-way trip.” — Spoilers for Mass Effect: Andromeda follow.




If Pathfinder Ryder settles the Taerve Uni outpost on Voeld, Kahele becomes a merchant for the colony. He sells weapon mods and minerals and materials.
