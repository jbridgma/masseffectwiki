Prerequisite: Priority: The Citadel II (Mass Effect 3)


Orisoni is composed of four worlds, three of which are terrestrials while one is a gas giant. Egalic has eezo resources coveted by the Elkoss Combine, but negotiations with the asari stalled in light of the Reaper invasion. As only Niacal is habitable, the Reapers focused their attacks on this world.
