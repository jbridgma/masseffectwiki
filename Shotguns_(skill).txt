This article is about the skill in Mass Effect: Andromeda. For shotgun weapons in Mass Effect, Mass Effect 2, Mass Effect 3, and Mass Effect: Andromeda, see Shotguns.

Shotguns is a passive Combat skill in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Skill Ranks[]
Specialized training for close-quarters combatants. Shotguns deal high damage at short range at the expense of poor accuracy at long distances.

Player Notes[]
Mechanics
Shotguns is a passive skill that is always active.
Strategies
The Rank 5 Reload upgrade does not affect the cooldown period of a weapon that uses the Vintage Heat Sink.
