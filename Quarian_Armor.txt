


Quarian Armor is worn by quarians in Mass Effect. Only light Quarian Armor is seen in-game.
In Bring Down the Sky, Commander Shepard mentions to Simon Atwell that quarian armor is particularly hard to come by. Its rarity is likely due to the fact that quarians rarely leave the Flotilla.
