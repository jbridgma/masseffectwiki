Ian Ferguson[1] is a cut character who was present in Mass Effect on the SSV Normandy. He may have been related to the cut assignment Global Plot 1, though no further details are known about his involvement.[2]
Ian Ferguson is one of the Normandy's crewmen posted in the cargo hold. He's from Earth and harbors a distinct Scottish accent. During the only recovered conversation between him and Shepard, he mentions his concerns about the presence of Urdnot Wrex, which Shepard quickly dismisses.
