Fluorite (F) is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A mineral used for creating precision scientific lenses, among other applications.

Acquisition[]
Fluorite can be detected via orbital scan and may be discovered in small amounts on foot. It can also be purchased from certain merchants. 
Planets where fluorite can be found on foot:

Elaaden
Eos
Voeld
Specific Uses[]
Fluorite is used for a medium number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Fluorite listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
