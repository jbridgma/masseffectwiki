Description[]
Vinland is a small rocky world with a "pressure-cooker" atmosphere composed of argon and carbon dioxide. Its valuable cobalt deposits would require robotic mining due to volatile surface gases.

Trivia[]
The planet is named after the North American region of the same name, which was visited by Lief Eriksson, the system's namesake.
