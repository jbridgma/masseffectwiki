Geth Infiltrator is a passive class power in Mass Effect 2.


Augments Legion's combat skills and weapon damage; improves its power recharge time

Power Ranks[]
Rank 1 - Legion continually upgrades its combat matrices, improving its health, weapon damage, and the recharge time of its powers.

Weapon Damage: +6.00%

Power Recharge Time: -12.00%
Rank 3 - Legion continually upgrades its combat matrices, improving its health, weapon damage, and the recharge time of its powers.
Health: +15.00%
Weapon Damage: +18.00%
Power Recharge Time: -18.00%

Rank 4
Evolves Geth Infiltrator into one of the following options:

Geth Assassin[]
Legion has become an expert at disassembling organics and synthetics alike, increasing its weapon damage.
Health: +15.00%

Geth Trooper[]
