






This article is about the Mass Effect 2 Infiltrator class power. For the Mass Effect specialization, see Operative. For the Mass Effect 3 version, see Operational Mastery.

Operative is a passive class power in Mass Effect 2.


Your weapon damage is more punishing and frequent, and targeting enemies is easier.

Bugs[]
There is a bug concerning the Sniper Time Slowdown. During the slowdown, timers (like those for the shutters in Dossier: Archangel) count down just as fast as they would without the time slowdown.
