This article is about the skill in Mass Effect: Andromeda. For assault rifle weapons in Mass Effect, Mass Effect 2, Mass Effect 3, and Mass Effect: Andromeda, see Assault Rifles.

Assault Rifles is a passive Combat skill in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Skill Ranks[]
Advanced training improves facility with assault rifles for dedicated combatants. Assault rifles offer moderate accuracy, range, and stopping power.

Player Notes[]
Mechanics
Assault Rifles is a passive skill that is always active.
Strategies
The Rank 5 Reload upgrade does not affect the cooldown period of a weapon that uses the Vintage Heat Sink.
