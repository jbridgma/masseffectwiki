


Omni-Bow Mastery is a passive class power in Mass Effect 3's multiplayer mode. 

Power Ranks[]
Rank 1: Omni-Bow Mastery[]
Boost health, shields, and omni-bow damage. Your omni-bow attacks are considered melee attacks and receive bonuses from melee upgrades. Concussive and armor-piercing arrows are considered power attacks and receive bonuses from power upgrades. While active, concussive and armor-piercing arrow damage supplements the base omni-bow damage.

Health & Shield Bonus: 15%
Omni-Bow Damage Bonus: 15%


Rank 2: Durability[]
Increase health and shield bonuses by 10%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25%
Omni-Bow Damage Bonus: 15%


Rank 3: Omni-Bow Damage[]
Increase omni-bow damage by 25%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25%
Omni-Bow Damage Bonus: 40%


Rank 4: Omni-Bow Damage/Durability[]



Omni-Bow Damage
Increase omni-bow damage by 35%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25%
Omni-Bow Damage Bonus: 75%


Durability
Increase health and shield bonuses by 15%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 40%
Omni-Bow Damage Bonus: 40%



Rank 5: Killing Spree/Shield Recharge[]



Killing Spree
Increase omni-bow damage by 25% for 30 seconds after an enemy is killed by your omni-bow.



Shield Recharge
Decrease shield-recharge delay by 15%.




Rank 6: Omni-Bow Damage/Fitness Expert[]



Omni-Bow Damage
Increase omni-bow damage by 50%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25% (Omni-Bow Damage), 40% (Durability)
Omni-Bow Damage Bonus: 125% (Omni-Bow Damage), 90% (Durability)


Fitness Expert
Increase health and shield bonuses by 25%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 50% (Omni-Bow Damage), 65% (Durability)
Omni-Bow Damage Bonus: 75% (Omni-Bow Damage), 40% (Durability)



Player Notes[]
Omni-Bow Mastery is considered a melee attack.
The Shotgun Omni-Blade will provide a greater damage bonus than a Pistol Melee Stunner and a Pistol Power Magnifier.[1]
Availability[]
Single-player: N/A
Multiplayer: Talon Mercenary Engineer
