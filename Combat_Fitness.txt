Player Notes[]
Mechanics
Combat Fitness is a passive skill that is always active.
Rank 3 Extra Holster adds 15 points of Weight Capacity, rather than %.
Rank 5 Extra Holster adds 25 points of Weight Capacity, rather than %.

Notes about the skill's strategies
Availability[]
Single-player: Pathfinder Ryder - Starting Combat Skill or Operative Training, Security Training, Scrapper Training
Multiplayer: Batarian Scrapper, Batarian Vanguard, Human Commando, Human Infiltrator, Human Soldier, Turian Soldier
