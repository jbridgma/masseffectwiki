Player Notes[]
Mechanics
While using the Pull Throw combo, the enemies or items held when thrown will take the properties of throw. i.e. they will arc and home in on enemies.
If an enemy is primed when pull thrown, it will trigger a combo detonation when the enemy reaches the target.

Notes about the skill's strategies
