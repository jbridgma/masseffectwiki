Mechs are a variety of machine constructs employed variously for combat purposes and/or heavy-duty locomotion. Automated models have some form of VI governing their behavior while larger mechs can have pilots directing them from built-in cockpits.

Atlas Mech - a piloted mech built by Cerberus and encountered in Mass Effect 3.
FENRIS Mech - a quadrupedal model equipped with a short-range taser encountered in Mass Effect 2.
Hydra Mech - a large, bipedal model in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
LOKI Mech - a bipedal model equipped with small arms encountered in Mass Effect 2.
Ogre Mech - a large, heavily-armed anti-infantry model encountered in Mass Effect: Infiltrator.
Orcus Mech - a bipedal model equipped with an assault rifle in Mass Effect: Infiltrator.
Rampart Mech - an upgraded LOKI Mech designed by Cerberus for crowd-control on Omega. It appears in Mass Effect 3: Omega.
Triton ADS - a mech that resembles the Atlas and was designed for deep sea exploration. It appears in Mass Effect 3: Leviathan.
