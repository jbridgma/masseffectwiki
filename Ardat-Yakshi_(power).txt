Ardat Yakshi is a passive class power in Mass Effect 2.

See also: Squad Members Guide (Mass Effect 2)#Samara/Morinth

Boosts Morinth's combat skills, weapon damage, and health; improves her power recharge time

Power Ranks[]
Rank 1 - Morinth's ability to feed off the dying gives her unnatural increases to her health, weapon damage, and the recharge time of her powers.
Health: +5.00%
Weapon Damage: +6.00%
Power Recharge Time: -6.00%
Rank 2 - Morinth's ability to feed off the dying gives her unnatural increases to her health, weapon damage, and the recharge time of her powers.
Health: +10.00%
Weapon Damage: +12.00%
Power Recharge Time: -12.00%
Rank 3 - Morinth's ability to feed off the dying gives her unnatural increases to her health, weapon damage, and the recharge time of her powers.
Health: +15.00%
Weapon Damage: +18.00%
Power Recharge Time: -18.00%

Rank 4
Evolves Ardat Yakshi into one of the following options:

Erdua Yakshi[]
Morinth's considerable stamina further increases her health and reduces the recharge time of her powers.
Health: +20.00%
Weapon Damage: +18.00%
Power Recharge Time: -25.00%
Malian Yakshi[]
Morinth's consumption of lives further increases her health as well as her weapon damage.
Health: +20.00%
Weapon Damage: +25.00%
Power Recharge Time: -18.00%
