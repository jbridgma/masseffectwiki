The Vanquisher is a sniper rifle in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Description[]
The Vanquisher is a versatile sniper rifle that packs a solid punch. Built by Nexus exiles, it is highly modular and damage-resistant-its effectiveness is unimpeded by sandstorms, snow, and other weather conditions.

Features[]
The Vanquisher is widely considered one of the best sniper rifles in Mass Effect: Andromeda due to its relatively high power, extremely high rate of fire, and extremely high accuracy. For a sniper rifle, it also has a large clip and a lot of reserve ammo.
In multiplayer, the Vanquisher has seen numerous adjustments due to overreliance on the weapon. As of the very last update, it has a net increase in damage and rate of fire. Its reload time has been reduced from flat 2.5 seconds to 2.5-2.4 seconds depending on rank. However, its weight has also been increased from 45-23 to 55-28.
