Description[]
Ma Huan is largely composed of hydrogen and methane. Its relative proximity to the Nexus makes it a useful core discharge site. Plans for a helium-3 skimming station are in progress.

Trivia[]
Ma Huan is named for one of the navigators who accompanied the Chinese explorer Zheng He on his journeys.
