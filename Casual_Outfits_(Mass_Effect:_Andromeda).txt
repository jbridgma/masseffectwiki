Casual Outfits are worn by Pathfinder Ryder aboard by the Hyperion, Nexus, Tempest, and during specific cutscenes. 

Overview[]
Casual Outfits can be changed and customized in the Wardrobe terminal in the Pathfinder's quarters aboard the Tempest. 

Outfits[]
The following Casual Outfits are available to the player:

Hoodie - Deluxe and Super Deluxe Editions
Jacket
Long Sleeves
Pathfinder Casual Outfit - Deluxe and Super Deluxe Edition
