







The following information is drawn from cut content and is not to be considered canon in any way.

Citadel: The Mugger is an assignment in Mass Effect which was cut during development. It would have taken place on the Citadel, and involved dealing with a mugger.[1]

Plotline[]
This assignment apparently consisted of dealing with a mugger, which could be accomplished in two ways: either persuade the mugger (Charm or Intimidate), or they must be killed.
