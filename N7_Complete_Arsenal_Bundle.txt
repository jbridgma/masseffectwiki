


The N7 Complete Arsenal Bundle is a DLC bundle pack for Mass Effect 2 released on January 18, 2011, and is only available on PlayStation Network.

Content[]
Alternate Appearance Pack 1
Equalizer Pack
Aegis Pack
Firepower Pack
Terminus Weapon and Armor
Recon Operations Pack
Pricing[]
The pack is available on PlayStation Network for $7.99 USD.
