


Gladiator Armor is produced by Elkoss Combine. It provides light to moderate protection. It is also  Liara's default armor. It uses reverse-engineered technology, causing a drop in the efficiency of its shields as well as of its biotic/tech damage protection when compared to most armors made by competitors.[1]
