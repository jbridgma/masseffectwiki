Description[]
Gong Zhen is a small but scorching world, partially due to a crushing nitrogen-carbon dioxide atmosphere. Evidence suggests its icy ring was once a small comet.

Trivia[]
Gong Zhen was a fifteenth-century translator and writer who accompanied the Chinese explorer Zheng He on his journeys.
