






This article is about the line of turian armor in Mass Effect. For the Cerberus unit in Mass Effect 3, see Phantom.


Phantom Armor is manufactured by the Serrice Council and can only be worn by turians. It is also Nihlus Kryik's signature armor. It was made out of asari materials, which provide it with excellent stats.[1]
