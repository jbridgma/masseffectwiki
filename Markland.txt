Description[]
Markland is a large frozen world, with a surface composed mostly of silica with deposits of cobalt. The planet's albedo is unexpectedly dark, suggesting geological activity or contamination from the Scourge.

Trivia[]
The moon is named after the North American region of the same name, which was visited by Leif Eriksson, the system's namesake.
