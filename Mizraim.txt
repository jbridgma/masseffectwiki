Description[]
A small gas giant, Mizraim is primarily hydrogen and methane around a rocky core. There is no remaining trace of the civilization from Joab on Mizraim itself, but debris orbiting the planet indicates that artificial satellites were once in place before being destroyed.

Trivia[]
Mizraim is named after the grandson of the biblical figure Noah. It is also the Hebrew name for the land of Egypt.
