Description[]
One of a trio of asteroids formed around an element zero core, Farlas is the easiest to mine for low-yield eezo. A carbonaceous asteroid, Farlas has a trace of water-bearing minerals and organic carbon in the form of kerogen. Currently, the asteroid is surrounded by quarian mining ships extracting fuel for the flotilla.
