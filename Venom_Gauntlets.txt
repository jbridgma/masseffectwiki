


Venom Gauntlets is a passive class power in Mass Effect 3's multiplayer mode.

Power Ranks[]
Rank 1: Venom Gauntlets[]
Boost health, shields, melee damage, and durability.
30% of melee damage is applied as poison damage over 5 seconds.

Health & Shield Bonus: 25%
Melee Damage Bonus: 15%


Rank 2: Durability[]
Increase health and shield bonuses by 15%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 40%
Melee Damage Bonus: 15%


Rank 3: Melee Damage[]
Increase melee damage bonus by 20%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 40%
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%

Melee Damage
Increase melee damage bonus by 30%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 40%
Melee Damage Bonus: 65%


Durability
Increase health and shield bonuses by 20%.

Health & Shield Bonus: 60%
Melee Damage Bonus: 35%

Martial Artist
Increase melee damage bonus by 75% for 30 seconds after an enemy is killed by a heavy melee.



Shield Recharge

Melee Synergy
