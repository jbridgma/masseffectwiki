The Isharay is a sniper rifle in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Description[]
This heavy angaran sniper rifle uses an entire thermal clip with each shot. Slow to reload, but it strikes with massive force. The Isharay's name translates as "goodbye," and the weapon has few rivals when it comes to raw power. The angara take understandable pride in its effectiveness.  

Features[]
The shell filaments required for the singleplayer Rank X Isharay are very high and doesn't follow the small increasing amounts of shell filaments per Rank.

Strategies[]
Mods with penalties to clip size like the SR Calibrated Receiver and SR Latticed Materials can be taken freely. The penalties can be ignored since the weapon is at the bare minimum clip size needed to function.
