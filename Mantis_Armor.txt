






This article is about the armor set. For the sniper rifle, see M-92 Mantis. For the gunship, see A-61 Mantis Gunship.

Mantis Armor is produced by Hahne-Kedar. It provides light to moderate protection. Like all other armors of the same manufacturer, its field destabilizer results in heightened shield efficiency in comparison to competitors.[1]
