Prerequisite: Collector Ship (mission) or Project Firewalker: Rosalie Lost and Horizon (mission) (Mass Effect 2)

Talava is distinguished by the presence of a turian prison planet, patrols seeking to discourage jailbreaks from the planet, and Terminus Systems criminals looking for a staging ground. All of its worlds are inhospitable, and barely exploited by commercial mining interests.
