Description[]
A small rock planet, Makhaira's thin atmosphere and high albedo keeps it from being much hotter than it is. The crust is high in sodium oxide, giving the planet a whitish tinge.

Trivia[]
A makhaira is a type of knife with a slight backwards curve. It was commonly used in ancient Greece, with the general and historian Xenophon recommending its use by cavalry soldiers.
