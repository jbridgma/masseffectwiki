


Mass Effect 3: N7 Warfare Gear is a DLC pack for Mass Effect 3. It was available as a retail bonus item to players who pre-ordered the game from certain retailers.[1] It is also included in the Wii U version of Mass Effect 3.[2][3][4] It is currently available on PC as part of the Mass Effect 3 DLC Bundle.

Content[]
Two assault rifles:
N7 Valkyrie
M-55 Argus
One armor set:
N7 Defender Armor
