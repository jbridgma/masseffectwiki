


The Mass Effect: Paragon Lost Original Motion Picture Score was made available to the public on November 21, 2012.[1] 26 of its 27 tracks were composed by Joshua Mosley and David Kates, and the 27th is a song by The Anix. The soundtrack is currently available on iTunes.[2] and Amazon.[3]
