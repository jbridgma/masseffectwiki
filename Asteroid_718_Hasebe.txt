Asteroid 718 Hasebe

Region
Milky Way / Artemis Tau / Athens


Location: Milky Way / Artemis Tau / Athens System / Proteus
Asteroid 718 Hasebe is a cut location which would have been where the events of UNC: Bring Down the Sky take place. Its spiritual successor in X57: Bring Down the Sky is Asteroid X57.

Description[]
Hasebe is a rocky asteroid with no atmosphere and little gravity. It has been equipped with three crude fusion torches, which are being used to move it into the orbit of the nearby planet Proteus. On its current trajectory it will actually strike the colony.[1]
