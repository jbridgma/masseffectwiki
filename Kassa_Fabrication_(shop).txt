Kassa Fabrication is located on the Presidium Commons of the Citadel. The terminal is found on the right shop at the lower level of the Meridian Place.







“There won't be an Earth left to save...” — Spoilers for Mass Effect 3 follow.




Notes[]
It's possible to purchase items from this shop through the Alliance Requisitions Procurement terminal in the Normandy's shuttle bay following Priority: The Citadel I, even though it can't be physically visited until after Priority: Palaven.
Because the shop is inaccessible after Priority: Cerberus Headquarters, the M-37 Falcon, if missed during the mission, can only be purchased through the terminal in the Normandy's shuttle bay.
