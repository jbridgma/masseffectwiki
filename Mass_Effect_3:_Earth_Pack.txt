






This article is about the multiplayer DLC pack for Mass Effect 3. For the human homeworld, see Earth.


Mass Effect 3: Earth is a DLC pack for Mass Effect 3. It was released on July 17, 2012 on Xbox 360 and PC worldwide. For Playstation 3, it was released on July 17, 2012 in North America, and on July 18, 2012 in Europe. The Earth pack is available at no additional cost.[1] It is included with copies of Mass Effect 3: Special Edition for Wii U.[2]

Content[]
Three maps:
Firebase Rio
Firebase Vancouver
Firebase London
Six multiplayer characters:
N7 Fury Adept
N7 Destroyer Soldier
N7 Demolisher Engineer
N7 Paladin Sentinel
N7 Shadow Infiltrator
N7 Slayer Vanguard
Three weapons:
Acolyte Pistol
N7 Piranha Shotgun
N7 Typhoon Assault Rifle
Five1 Weapon Mods:
Assault Rifle Omni-Blade
Pistol Cranial Trauma System
Pistol Ultralight  Materials
SMG High-Velocity Barrel
SMG Recoil System
Eleven gear upgrades
One match objective:
Teams must protect and escort a Combat Drone to a designated extraction zone. This objective randomly occurs during waves 3, 6, and 10.[3]
One new difficulty setting:
On Platinum difficulty, the selected enemy faction will appear at full force immediately. As the match progresses, enemies from other factions will begin appearing.[3]
1 It was initially reported that twelve weapon mods would be included in Earth, however, seven of them were removed due to balance issues. BioWare released the missing mods in a patch released alongside Mass Effect 3: Retaliation.[4]
