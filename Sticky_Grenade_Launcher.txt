Compatibilities[]
Guns - This augmentation can be applied to gun-type weapons.
Description[]
Non-shotgun weapons: Each projectile is now a sticky grenade
Shotgun: Each pellet is now a sticky grenade, with lower damage per pellet 

Mechanics[]
The Sticky Grenade Launcher augmentation SHOULD NOT be combined with the Plasma Charge System. The Plasma Charge system interferes with the functionality of the Sticky Grenade Launcher.
