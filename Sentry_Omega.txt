Trivia[]
Omega is the final letter of the Greek alphabet and is often used metonymically to mean 'last' or 'final'. Sentry is a guardian or a gate, so the cluster's name can be taken to mean "last guard" or "final guard" as Virmire, one of the last mission worlds, is in this cluster.
