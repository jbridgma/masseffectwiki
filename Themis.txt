Description[]
A hydrogen-helium gas giant, Themis is one of Arcturus's stable satellites. It rapidly orbits the massive star.

Trivia[]
Issue One of Mass Effect: Foundation takes place in an unnamed asteroid-mining facility in Themis' orbit.
