Omni-Gel Canister is a collectible resource in Mass Effect: Andromeda.

Analysis[]
A semi-molten mixture of alloys and synthetic polymers used in Milky Way technology for rapid fabrication. 

Acquisition[]
Omni-gel canisters are often found on defeated Milky Way exiles, or acquired by dismantling exile weapons and armor. They can also be purchased from certain merchants. 

Specific Uses[]
Omni-Gel Canister is used for a huge number of items in Mass Effect: Andromeda.
The total amount of Omni-Gel Canister listed is for one crafted copy of each item.
