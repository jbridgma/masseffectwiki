Remnant Armor is a passive Tech skill in Mass Effect: Andromeda Multiplayer.

Skill Ranks[]
Experimental power armor that enhances mobility, melee attacks, and defense. Remnant Armor "converts" wasted energy from weapons, powers, and melee attacks to improve its augmentations as you deal damage.
