Memories of Illium is a shop on Illium. It features models of the Alliance Cruiser, Athabasca Class Freighter and the UT-47 Kodiak Drop Shuttle.

Discount[]
To obtain the discount, speak to the asari shopkeeper, Ereba, standing beside the store and resolve her problem with her krogan boyfriend.
