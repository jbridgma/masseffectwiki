Compatibilities[]
Chest - This augmentation can be applied to Chest armor pieces.
Guns - This augmentation can be applied to gun-type weapons.
Description[]
(Weapon) When current clip is full: +20% Power Damage
(Chest) When health is 30% or less: +25% Power Status Effect Duration

Mechanics[]
Strategies[]
This augmentation is best placed on Remnant weapons or weapons with the Vintage Heat Sink augmentation, as they automatically reload.
